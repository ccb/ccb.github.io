Mobile Support on iOS
View sharing is used all the time on desktop computers to share work or to get help with a problem. But view sharing isn't really possible on a mobile device. Sandboxing of the app makes it impossible for one app to capture views from another and Apple hasn't provided a screen sharing implementation. Mobile Support allows app developers to add screen sharing specifically to their app.

Hands Free Medical Visualization
Nurses and doctors often need to access medical images while performing surgery. But if they use a mouse or touch an iPad they need to resterilize. This makes working with medical imaging during surgery tedious and awkward. By using a Leap Motion sensor to find the position and motion of a users hand, they can zoom and slice through MRI or CT scans.

Multisurface Systems
A multisurface system is a collection of different devices all working closely together. One of the sources of friction in such multi-device environments is transfering content between devices. Traditionally this was done using menus or lists which are awkward and unintuitive. MSE-API allows developers to build systems where content can be transferred using physical gestures which map to the spatial layout of the room. This means that to send a file, you just need to point at your target device and "flick" the content over.
